The Compass

A compass is a navigation tool used to find direction. It works by using a magnetized needle that aligns with the Earth's magnetic field, pointing toward the North Pole.

Why is the Compass Important?

  • Helps travelers find direction
  • Used in navigation at sea and on land
  • Important for explorers and hikers
  • Still used as a backup when GPS fails

Types of Compasses

  • Magnetic Compass
  • Digital Compass
  • Gyrocompass
